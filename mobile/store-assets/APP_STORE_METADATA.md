### Full Description
BrindaWorld empowers girls aged 6-14 to discover who they can become.

**WHAT YOUR DAUGHTER WILL LEARN:**
- Chess — play against an age-adaptive AI opponent
- Coding — drag-and-drop and text coding adventures
- Geography — explore all 195 countries
- She Can Be — quiz 12 professions with real Canadian women role models
- Confidence — evidence-based activities to build self-esteem
- Leadership — age-appropriate leadership and citizenship content

**SHE CAN BE ANYTHING:**
Meet Roberta Bondar (Astronaut), Kim Campbell (Prime Minister), Donna Strickland (Nobel Prize Scientist), Jennie Carignan (Chief of Defence Staff), and 8 more incredible Canadian women.

**PARENT FEATURES:**
- Progress dashboard
- Up to 6 child profiles on Family plan
- Weekly summary emails
- Teacher portal integration

**FOR TEACHERS:**
Free teacher accounts include class management, student progress tracking, and NB curriculum-aligned content.

**BILINGUAL:**
Full English and French support.

**PRIVACY:**
COPPA and PIPEDA compliant. Children's data never sold.
