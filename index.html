💎 Girls Who Changed the World!

Meet the incredible women who made history — and inspire us all!

🚀
Hero #1

Valentina Tereshkova

In 1963, Valentina became the first woman to travel to space! She orbited Earth 48 times in just 3 days. She proved that girls truly can reach the stars! 🌟

⚕️
Hero #2

Marie Curie

Marie was the first person to win two Nobel Prizes — in Physics AND Chemistry! She discovered two elements and changed science forever. Learning is a superpower! 🔬

✊
Hero #3

Malala Yousafzai

Malala fought for every girl's right to go to school. She became the youngest Nobel Peace Prize winner at age 17. Her voice changed the world! 📚

💻
Hero #4

Ada Lovelace

Ada wrote the world's very first computer program in the 1800s — before computers even existed! She is the mother of computing. Girls invented tech! 💡

🌍
Hero #5

Wangari Maathai

Wangari planted 47 million trees across Africa to fight climate change and won the Nobel Peace Prize. One person really can save the planet! 🌱

🎵
Hero #6

Marian Anderson

Marian became one of the greatest singers in history and broke barriers for Black women in classical music. Her voice opened doors for millions! 🎶

⚖️
Hero #7

Ruth Bader Ginsburg

Ruth fought for equal rights for women and men in the law courts her entire life. She showed that fairness is worth fighting for every single day! ⚖️

🏃
Hero #8

Kathrine Switzer

In 1967, Kathrine became the first woman to officially run the Boston Marathon — even when officials tried to stop her. She kept running! 💪

👑
Hero #9

Brinda — Future Leader

This website was built for a 5-year-old girl named Brinda who loves to play and learn. One day, she will lead BrindaWorld — and maybe the whole world! 🌍💖
